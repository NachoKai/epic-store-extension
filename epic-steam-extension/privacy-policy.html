Privacy Policy

Steam Info Overlay for Epic (Unofficial) — Last updated: June 18, 2026

This extension is designed with privacy in mind. It does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personally identifiable information.

Information we collect

None. The extension does not collect names, emails, browsing history, analytics, or any personal data, and it does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

Local storage

The only data the extension saves is your selected price region (for example, "us" or "de"). This preference is stored locally in your browser using the Chrome storage API so the extension remembers your choice between visits. It never leaves your device and is not shared with anyone.

Network requests

When you view a product page on the Epic Games Store, the extension requests publicly available information from Steam's public store endpoints (review scores, pricing, and game details) in order to display it. It sends only the game title and your selected region as part of these lookups. No personal information is included in these requests.

Third-party services

Game data is retrieved from Steam (operated by Valve Corporation). Your use of that data is subject to Steam's own terms and privacy practices. This extension is independent and unofficial.

Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer any data to outside parties.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a revised date.
